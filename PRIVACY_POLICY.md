# Privacy Policy for S3 Explorer

**Last Updated: February 18, 2026**

## Overview

S3 Explorer ("the App") is developed by Abhishek Chavan. This privacy policy explains how the App handles your data.

## Data Collection

**S3 Explorer does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to external servers.**

The App operates entirely on your local device and connects only to Amazon Web Services (AWS) S3 endpoints that you configure.

## Information You Provide

To use the App, you may provide:
- AWS Access Key ID
- AWS Secret Access Key
- AWS Region preferences
- S3 bucket names and paths

**This information is stored locally on your device** in encrypted form and is never transmitted to any server other than AWS S3 endpoints for the purpose of accessing your S3 buckets.

## Data Storage

- All credentials and preferences are stored locally on your Mac using secure system storage
- No data is sent to third-party analytics or tracking services
- No personal information is collected or shared

## Third-Party Services

The App connects only to:
- **Amazon Web Services (AWS) S3** - to access your S3 buckets using credentials you provide

The App does not integrate with any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Data Security

- Credentials are stored securely using macOS Keychain or encrypted local storage
- All connections to AWS use HTTPS/TLS encryption
- No credentials are logged or transmitted outside of AWS API calls

## Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13 years of age.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above.

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal data to access, modify, or delete. Your AWS credentials remain entirely under your control and can be removed from the App at any time.
